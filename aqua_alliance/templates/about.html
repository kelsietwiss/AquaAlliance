About the Tulalip Wood Water Association


The Tulalip Wood Water Association, founded in 1972, is a non-profit association incorporated pursuant to the laws of the State of Washington.


The mission of the Association is to provide access to a clean and safe water system for the benefit of the members of the Association through the management of the shared well resource and associated infrastructure. The purpose of this site is to share information regarding the well, its infrastructure, and other water-related resources.
